The system vision is a joint vision of the system agreed upon all active stakeholders(Users developers shareholders)

3 part way
Problem: what exactly is the issue to be fixed 
solution: the rough/core idea how to solve the issue
Value: what added value comes with the solution

Problem: System vision of the Corona Warn App: The aim of the Corona-Warn-App is to identify SARS-CoV-2 chains of infection as quickly as possible and to break them.

Solution: It does this by rapidly and reliably notifying people that they have had contact with infected persons and therefore may have been exposed to the virus.
Value: These individuals can then self-isolate, to help stop the SARS-CoV-2 pandemic.
